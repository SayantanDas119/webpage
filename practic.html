ABOUT ME

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist,
philanthropist and former chairman of Tata Sons.
He was a chairman of the Tata Group from 1990
to 2012, and interim chairman from October
2016 through February 2017. He
continues to head its charitable trusts
